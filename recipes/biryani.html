Recipe Of Biryani.

Description:

Biryani is prepared from rice mixed with several spices and cooked in a special way.

Biryani, Mutton Biryani and Veg Biryani are some of the variants of this delicious dish.

Ingredient:

  • Chicken.
  • Rice.
  • Onions.
  • Spices.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Oil.

Steps:

  1. Add oil to a pot or pressure cooker and heat it.
  2. Add onions and fry till they turn golden.
  3. Next add chicken and fry till the color slightly changes to pale.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, red chili powder, coriander leaves and mint.
  5. Cook till the mixture turns thick and dry.
