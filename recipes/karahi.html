Recipe Of Karahi.

Description:

It is noted for its spicy taste and is notable in Pakistani and North Indian cuisine.

The dish is prepared in a karahi (a type of wok) and can take between 30 and 50 minutes.

Ingredient:

  • Chicken.
  • Garlic.
  • Onions.
  • Spices.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Green Chilli + Coriander
  • Oil.
  • Yogurt.
  • Mint.

Steps:

  1. Heat up your oil in a karahi dish, wok or pan suitable for stirfrying, keeping the flame on high for the entire duration.
  2. Add the chicken in it.
  3. Add in the minced ginger and garlic.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, red chili powder, coriander leaves and mint.
  5. Add all the chopped tomatoes and spices.
  6. Continue to cook this mix for about 20 minutes.
