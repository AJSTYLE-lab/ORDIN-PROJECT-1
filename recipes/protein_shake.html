Recipe Of Protein Shake.

Description:

People drink protein shakes for multiple reasons, including muscle gain, weight loss, and injury recovery.

protein shakes provide amino acids, also known as the building blocks of proteins.

Ingredient:

  • Banana.
  • Vanilla protein powder.
  • Almond milk.
  • Cinnamon.
  • Vanilla extract or scraped vanilla bean.
  • Chia seeds or flaxseed
  • Ice.

Steps:

  1. Add oil to a pot or pressure cooker and heat it.
  2. Scoop (25g) vanilla protein powder.
  3. 3/4 cup almond milk..
  4. 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
  5. 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract or scraped vanilla bean.
  6. 1/2 Tablespoon chia seeds or flaxseed, optional.
  7. Handful of ice.
